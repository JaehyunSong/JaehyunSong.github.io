Jaehyun Song, Ph.D.

Ph.D. in Political Science

I am an associate professor in Faculty of Informatics, Kansai University. I earned my Ph.D. and master degree in political science from Kobe University, and a bachelor degree in law from Keio University. My research interests are voting behavior, experimental political science, causal inference, and quantitative methodology. My CV is available here.
